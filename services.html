SERVICES & RATES



What we offer...

It is our aim to provide your dog with a positive experience whether thats an individual or group walk. This means if you would prefer one-on-one time spent with your dog, then we are very happy to oblige. Equally if they love to play and socialise then we can take them in small groups (max 5 dogs).

Dogs are always walked in areas that we are familiar with, areas that are safe and never by busy roads. No matter what the weather, you can guarantee a full hour of fun and exercise for your dog.

For your domestic pets and home visit requirements, we can offer a professional and confidential service for you and your animals. We will work with you to devise an individual schedule, so that your pets receive the attention and care that you would provide yourself and your pet deserves.

Please note: Bitches in season cannot be walked in groups. Individual walks can be arranged as required
